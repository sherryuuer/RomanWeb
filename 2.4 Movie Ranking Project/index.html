My favourite movies

Here are the best movies according to Sally

3 Idiots

This Bollywood film is a comedy-drama that follows the story of three friends who meet at an engineering college in India. It explores their adventures, challenges, and their pursuit of their individual dreams. "3 Idiots" is known for its humor, emotional depth, and its message about pursuing one's passion in life.

The Shawshank Redemption

Directed by Frank Darabont, this film is an adaptation of a Stephen King novella. It tells the story of a man who is wrongfully convicted of murder and his experiences in Shawshank State Penitentiary. The film explores themes of hope, friendship, and redemption and is widely regarded as one of the greatest movies ever made.

Gump

Starring Tom Hanks, this film follows the life of Forrest Gump, a man with a low IQ, as he inadvertently influences numerous historical events in the United States. The movie is a heartwarming tale of love, resilience, and the American experience.

Interstellar

Directed by Christopher Nolan, "Interstellar" is a science fiction epic that explores space travel, time dilation, and the survival of humanity. It follows a group of astronauts who embark on a journey through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity.

Avengers: Endgame

This Marvel Cinematic Universe film is the culmination of several superhero storylines. The Avengers assemble to undo the devastating effects of Thanos' snap, which wiped out half of all life in the universe. It's a thrilling and emotional conclusion to the Avengers saga.

The Matrix

Directed by the Wachowskis, "The Matrix" is a groundbreaking science fiction film that explores the concept of a simulated reality. It follows the story of Neo, a computer hacker who discovers the truth about the world he lives in and joins a group of rebels fighting against intelligent machines.
